About Me

I am a graphic designer and web developer with one year of professional experience creating visually compelling designs and functional, responsive websites. My background in graphic design provides me with a strong foundation in layout, typography, and branding, while my web development skills allow me to transform creative concepts into interactive, user-friendly digital experiences.

I am passionate about continuous growth and actively expanding my skill set to stay current with evolving design trends, coding practices, and technologies. Whether working on a website, digital asset, or brand identity, I strive to balance aesthetics with usability to deliver results that connect with audiences and meet project goals.

As I continue to learn and refine my craft, my goal is to take on projects that challenge me creatively and technically, while providing meaningful value to clients and users.
